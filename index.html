Who We Are

Teamwork, commitment & community.

FreightCenter was founded to help businesses and individuals ship easier & smarter with technology and time-saving tools. With the best, most innovative shipping technology platform around, we are on a mission to make shipping easier for everyone, and we proudly wear the hat of “shipping nerd.”

Our Culture - #FCLIFE

See what life is like at FreightCenter. It’s pretty awesome!

Our backbone is our dedicated shipping experts who provide our customers with the guidance they need. We are a group of hard-working individuals who believe in the motto “why can’t you have fun and be the best at what you do?” Our relaxed culture focuses on creativity, ambition and the individual successes of our team members. We encourage everyone to bring options, thoughts and ideas for ways that we can improve and deliver a better experience to our customers.   

  • Balance. The cliché “Work Hard, Play Hard.” YEP, we agree!
  • Team. You’re a key piece of the puzzle where amazing things
    happen.
  • Healthy Lifestyle. Break room with healthy choices and great snacks (PB&Js all day long).
  • Fun. Relax, take a break and clear your mind in the game room!
  • Events. We can honestly say you won’t be bored. From happy hours, fan days, local parades to donation drives, we always have something going on!
